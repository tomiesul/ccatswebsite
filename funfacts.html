Whisker Magic

Cat whiskers are incredibly sensitive, helping them
navigate and detect changes in their environment.
They're about as wide as their body, aiding in maneuvering
through tight spaces without getting stuck.



Purr Therapy

Cats' purring isn't just a sign of contentment;
it's also believed to have healing properties.
The frequency of a cat's purr, around 25 to 150 Hertz,
is thought to promote bone density and even help with pain relief.



Mysterious Communication

Cats have a diverse range of vocalizations,
from the famous "meow" to chirps, trills, and even purrs.
They use this repertoire to communicate with humans and other cats,
expressing a variety of emotions and needs.
